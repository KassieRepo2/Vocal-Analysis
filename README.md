# Vocal Analysis 
##### Developer: Kassie Whitney
## 1) Click New File to open your own recording
#### The only audio format that is currently in support is .wav


## 2) Click Record to make a new recording for your convenience
#### Stoping the recording and starting the recording will create new recordings. The vocal analyzer will only analyze the most recent recording. The previous recording will be saved. 

## 3) After your sample has been analyzed, the analysis will automatically appear keeping open the recording window.
